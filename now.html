Now

Currently in Quebec City, Canada, working full-time at Volta studio. In my free time, I draw personal art, make assets for other artists and work on a prototype of a mobile game with my friend Ondrej Barta.

Besides that, I think about the hidden costs of being far from home, the socio-economic and ecological impact of NFT and education in the post-covid world.

I say no to buying new things. Hello, minimalism and saving money. I also miss Czechia.
