Photobashing

I've seen many professional concept artists and illustrators going directly to Pinterest or Google Images for pictures not caring for a licence. I believe we can do better:

  • Unsplash, Pexels - Free photos for commecial use
  • Public domain photos on Flickr - From time to time hidden gems
  • Public Domain collections on Sketchfab - Public domain models
  • PurePNG - Transparent CC0 Library
