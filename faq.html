FAQ about my events

Published on Jan 3, 2025

How many people do you expect?

I’ve hosted as few as five people and as many as over twenty. However, since I just moved here, I expect fewer attendees. Building a proper community takes time. What matters is the quality of the conversation, not the quantity.

Can I invite a friend?

Yes, if they are someone you wouldn’t mind spending time with on a deserted island. :)

Do I need to bring anything?

I’ll provide tea, tasty drinks, and healthy canapés. You can bring something worth sharing, especially if you enjoy cooking or baking. Please avoid bringing junk food – we’re better than that.

How do I know that people there will be cool?

Well... you don’t.

I curate my events and carefully choose who to invite, but I can be wrong sometimes. However, as I find you cool, eventually there will be some convergence.

Do I need to RSVP?

Not really. What matters is that you show up.

Here’s the corrected version: Generally, people who don’t show up or don’t answer eventually stop being invited. But that’s life – you can please some of the people, some of the time.

Anything else I should keep in mind?
  • Come here with an open mind.
  • Listen more.
  • Avoid stealing conversations.
