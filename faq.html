FAQ about my events

How many people do you expect?

I’ve hosted as few as five people and as many as over twenty. However, since I just moved here, I expect fewer attendees. Building a proper community takes time. What matters is the quality of the conversation, not the quantity.

Can I invite a friend?

A close friend or your partner is welcome!

Do I need to bring anything?

I’ll provide tea, drinks, and healthy canapés. Feel free to BYOD or bring something to share. But please, no junk food, we’re better than that.

How do I know that people there will be cool?

Well... you don’t.

I curate my events and carefully choose who to invite, but I can be wrong sometimes. However, as I find you cool, eventually there will be some convergence.

Do I need to RSVP?

Not really. What matters is that you show up.

Generally, people who don’t show up or don’t answer eventually stop being invited. But that’s life – you can please some of the people, some of the time.
