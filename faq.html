Frequently asked question about my events.

Published on Jan 2, 2025

How many people do you expect?

I hosted just five people but also over twenty. But as I just moved here, I expect less. It takes time to build a proper community. What matters is a quality of converstaion, not quantity.

Can I invite a friend?

The answer is Yes, if she/he is the person you would don't mind spend time on a deserted island. :) Your siginificant other is always welcome.

Do I need to bring anything?

I’ll provide tea, tasty drinks, and healthy canapés. You can bring anything worth sharing, especially if you like cooking / baking. Please, avoid brining junk food, we are better than that.

How do I know that people there will be cool?

Well, you don't. I currate my events and chose who to invite. For example, people who do not reply are eventually dropped. People who I find not compatible with my values also. But that is a life. You can please some of the poeple, some of the time.
